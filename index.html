🦷 BrightSmile Dental — AI-Powered Clinic Management System

Built an end-to-end AI automation system for a dental clinic using n8n, Google Gemini, Cal.com, and OpenEMR. The system features a conversational AI receptionist (Aria) that collects patient information, checks real-time availability, books appointments, creates patient records in an EHR system, and sends automated email notifications — all without human intervention.

🤖 AI receptionist with natural language understanding
📅 Real-time availability & appointment booking
🏥 Automatic EHR patient record creation (OpenEMR)
📧 Branded email confirmations with reschedule/cancel links
🔔 Automated notifications for cancellations & no-shows
⏰ 24-hour appointment reminder emails
🔒 HIPAA-ready architecture with HTTPS encryption and MFA
